Animals to adopt The first step in adopting an animal from the Sanctuary is to complete an online matchmaking survey, which will give us some information about what you are looking for in your next pet. To launch the survey, select the animal type you are interested in adopting:

  1. Dog
  2. Cat
  3. Bunny
  4. Parrot
  5. Horse
  6. Pig

An adoption specialist will email you within 48 hours to talk about the next steps. We recommend adding bestfriends.org to your list of safe senders so that our email does not end up in your junk or spam folder. If you have any questions, please include them in your survey and one of our adoption specialists will be happy to answer them. Thank you for your patience, understanding and continued support for the animals during this unprecedented time.
